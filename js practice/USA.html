Tribute to Prithvi Narayan Shah

Prithvi Narayan Shah (1723–1775) was the first King of unified Nepal and the founder of the Shah dynasty, ruling from 1743 to 1775. He played a key role in unifying the small, fragmented kingdoms into a single nation.

-

Known for his strategic prowess and determination, Prithvi Narayan Shah envisioned a united Nepal that would be resilient against external threats. His efforts laid the foundation for the modern nation we know today.

This tribute page is a small acknowledgment of his contributions and the lasting impact he had on the history and identity of Nepal.
