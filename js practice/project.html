Overview

Prithvi Narayan Shah (1723–1775) was the first King of unified Nepal. He was born in Gorkha and ascended to the throne in 1743. Known for his vision and leadership, he successfully united the small fragmented kingdoms into a single nation.

Legacy

Prithvi Narayan Shah's dedication to his people and his strategic military campaigns are remembered as pivotal in shaping the history of Nepal. His vision for a unified nation laid the foundation for the strong and independent country that Nepal is today.
